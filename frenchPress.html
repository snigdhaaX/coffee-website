Master the French Press

Step 1: Boil water and let it cool for 30 seconds
Step 2: Add 2 tablespoons of coarsely ground coffee per cup into the press
Step 3: Pour hot water over the grounds and stir gently
Step 4: Place the lid on and let it steep for 4 minutes
Step 5: Slowly press down the plunger
Step 6: Pour into your cup and enjoy that rich, bold flavor
