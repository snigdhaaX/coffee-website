Make an Espresso Shot

Your bold coffee journey starts here!

Let's Brew Some Magic

Step 1: Grab some freshly roasted coffee beans
Step 2: Grind them fine — think powdery but not dusty!
Step 3: Load 18–20g of ground coffee into your espresso portafilter
Step 4: Tamp it down firmly and evenly – channel your inner barista
Step 5: Pull the shot! You want 25–30 seconds of liquid gold
Step 6: Sip and enjoy your beautifully bold espresso
Espresso yourself – one sip at a time
