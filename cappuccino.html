Make Your Perfect Cappuccino

Step 1: Brew 1 shot of espresso — rich and bold!
Step 2: Pour cold milk into a frothing pitcher
Step 3: Froth it! You want lots of microfoam!
00:08
Step 4: Gently pour steamed milk over your espresso
Step 5: Spoon the foam on top generously — cloud vibes only!
Step 6: Sprinkle some cocoa/cinnamon & sip your joy
