Mocha Magic

When chocolate meets coffee, it's love at first sip!

Make Your Dreamy Mocha

Step 1: Brew 1 shot of espresso (or strong coffee)
Step 2: Add 2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup or cocoa mix
Step 3: Steam 1/2 cup of milk until warm and frothy
Step 4: Pour the chocolate + coffee mix into a cup
Step 5: Gently add steamed milk and stir well
Step 6: Top with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa
Sweet. Strong. So Mocha.
