Make a Creamy Latte

Let’s foam, steam, and pour our way to latte heaven!

Latte-Making Quest Begins!

Step 1: Brew a strong shot of espresso — the heart of your latte
Step 2: Pour cold milk into a pitcher (whole milk works best!)
Step 3: Time to steam! Heat milk until it's 65°C (not boiling) — wait for it...
00:10
Step 4: Swirl to mix foam and milk — silky smooth is the goal
Step 5: Pour milk into the espresso slowly... make a heart if you're feeling fancy
Step 6: Enjoy your latte masterpiece like the coffee artist you are!
