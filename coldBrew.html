Cold Brew Vibes

Chill out with the smoothest coffee in town!

Brew the Cool Way

Step 1: Coarsely grind your favorite coffee beans
Step 2: Mix with cold, filtered water in a 1:4 ratio
Step 3: Steep it sloooowly... for 12–18 hours in the fridge
Step 4: Strain it using a fine mesh or cheesecloth
Step 5: Serve over ice — add milk or sweetener if you like
Step 6: Sip your chill brew and feel the calm
Cool, calm, caffeinated.
